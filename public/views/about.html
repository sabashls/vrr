Tamilnadu Basketball Association

Tamilnadu Basketball Association or TNBA is the governing and controlling body of basketball in Tamilnadu, and is responsible for the development and promotion of the sport at all levels in the state. TNBA is affiliated to BFI (Basketball Federation of India), TNOA (Tamilnadu Olympic Association) and is recognized by SDAT (Sports Development Authority of Tamilnadu). TNBA headed by President: Mr.V.V.R.Raj Satyen.
